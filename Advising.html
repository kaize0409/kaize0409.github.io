Openings

Wanna be real researchers? I'm seeking highly motivated students/interns to join my REAL lab. More details about our PhD program can be found here. Some expectations for the applicants:

  • Passion for scientific research (motivation, curiosity, persistence, etc.)
  • At least 1 publication/submission (first author) at top AI conferences.
  • Good English written and speaking skills (e.g., TOEFL > 100, IELTS > 7.0).
  • Strong programming and mathematical foundations are preferred.
